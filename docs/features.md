# ElderEase AI

## Problem Statement

Many elderly individuals struggle with managing medicines, remembering schedules, and accessing quick assistance for day-to-day concerns. Family members may not always be available to provide continuous support. ElderEase AI aims to improve the independence, safety, and well-being of elderly users through intelligent reminders and AI-powered assistance.

---

## Project Objective

To develop an AI-powered elderly assistance platform that helps users manage medications, receive reminders, track daily activities, and interact with an intelligent assistant for guidance and support.
